Welcome to the official website of the Putnam Valey Federation of Teachers (PVFT), Local 1867, of the New York State United Teachers (NYSUT) and it's national affiliates.

The purpose of this organization shall be:

to advance the standards of the teaching profession.

to support a commitment to the geniune spirit of professional ethics.
