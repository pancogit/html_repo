Site Purpose

Our intranet website exists for many reasons. All documentation, trainings, employees information and many more on one location.

Every employee activity, company strategy is logged, so the basic overview is present. Monthly reports, payment data and other law things are tracked also. Marketing structure and organization are also very important part of our company, so must be tracked.

For any problems with company devices (computers, laptops, printers or some other components), please send a ticket and our IT support will solve these problems as soon as possible. Do not open emails which comes from outside of our company, because of sensitive data restrictions. Also, have a look to not save your passwords on intranet for security reasons.

Please visit intranet website at least once in few days to stay in tune with latest updates and news from our company. Don't hesitate to ask any question on our forum. Update your company CV at least once in four months, so our managers can have a better look at your skills for recruitment.

If you have any question, please contact our IT support and you will got solution in no time.
